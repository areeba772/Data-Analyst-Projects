Monday Coffee Sales Analysis
Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing coffee sales data to derive business insights for Monday Coffee. By examining customer behavior, city-wise sales performance, and market potential, this analysis helps in making data-driven decisions regarding expansion and marketing strategies.

Key Features
Database Design: Created a normalized relational database schema (Customers, Products, City, Sales) to manage transactional data.

Data Cleaning: Performed schema adjustments and ensured data integrity using foreign key constraints.

Business Intelligence: Developed complex SQL queries to solve real-world business problems, such as calculating market penetration and sales growth.

Key Business Insights & Queries
The project addresses several critical business questions:

Market Penetration: Calculated potential coffee consumers in each city (assuming 25% of the total population).

Revenue Analysis: Tracked quarterly revenue trends for 2023 to identify peak performance periods.

Product Performance: Identified top-selling products in each city using window functions (DENSE_RANK).

Operational Efficiency: Analyzed the "Average Rent per Customer" to identify cost-effective cities for business operations.

Growth Tracking: Calculated month-over-month sales growth ratios to monitor market stability.

Recommendations
Based on the data analysis, the following cities are recommended for strategic focus:

Pune: Represents the highest total revenue with an exceptionally low average rent per customer, making it a highly profitable market.

Delhi: Offers the largest market potential with approximately 7.7 million estimated coffee consumers and a strong existing customer base.

Jaipur: Features a high volume of unique customers and a very low average rent per customer (156), offering a great balance for scaling operations.

Technologies Used
SQL (MySQL/PostgreSQL): Used for database creation, data manipulation, and analytical querying.

Database Modeling: Normalized schema design for efficient data retrieval.

This project demonstrates foundational skills in SQL-based data analysis and business problem-solving
